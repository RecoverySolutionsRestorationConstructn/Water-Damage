Water Damage

If your home or business has experienced recent water damage, call the experts at Recovery Solutions as soon as possible to avoid long-lasting problems.

Whether it’s caused by a broken dishwasher or a natural disaster, water damage is known to impact around 14,000 people in the United States daily. When left untreated, water rots wood, rusts steel, de-laminates plywood and can leave hazardous mold throughout your home.

Read Post

At Recovery Solutions, our skilled technicians use a variety of water damage restoration equipment to remove excess water and prevent it from seeping further into your home. From pumps and vacuums to industrial-grade dehumidifiers, we work quickly and carefully to ensure your home is restored to its former condition.

Removing water from your home or business can be tricky, especially if you do not have the experience to do so. In addition to drying your floors, our restoration specialists will examine your carpet for signs of hidden water damage and use specialized deodorization tools to erase any powerful odors and prevent mold growth.
